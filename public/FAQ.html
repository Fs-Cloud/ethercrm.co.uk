Frequently Asked Questions

Where you can finf the answer to the most common questions.

Getting Started

I am unable to login to my client portal?

First please ensure that your CAPS LOCK on your keyboard is off as you may be inputting your details incorrectly. If this doesn't work try clearing your browser data or cookies to refresh the page. If you are still unable to login use the reset password button. A new password will be sent to the e-mail registed to your license. If you are still unable to login please contact our support team Contact

EtherCRM looks diffrent in the browser?

We aim to make EtherCRM cross-browser compatible for all platforms but due to constant updates this is not always possible. We recommend users use Internet Explorer or Google Chrome to acess the platform.

What APIs and functionality can developers use?

Our developers are currently working on this and it should be expected in Q2 of 2018.

Can users choose to install the app permanently?

No. Due to security and constant updates of client tennants, EtherCRM will be availible on-line only. By doing this we are able to preserve the overall efficiency and security of the system and avoid imcompatability issues.

Account

Can I get a price list?

All pricing and currently customised per customer based on their location, number of users, usage rates and security requirements. In the future we will slowly move towards standardised pricing model. Current costs of EtherCRM are for illustrative purposes only.

What countries doe EtherCRM operate in?

EtherCRM is a trading name of Binary01 Limited and is located in the UK. We currently have re-sellers worldwide and store client data within their contry of operation.

Can I cancel my EtherCRM contract/subscription?

Clients are free to cancel at anytime, subject to the cancellation clauses in their license agreements.

Can I get a free trial?

Free trails are only provided to those who booked and attended the EtherCRM demo.

Pricing & License

Where can I see the licenses that I bought?

You should be able to see your license through your 'client-dashboard'. You should also have an e-mail confirmign your purchase and licenses held.

Am I unable to continue using EtherCRM if my license expires?

You will be unable to use EtherCRM until you purchase a new license. Customers can activate a 24hr free temp-license at the end of their subscription to allow them to remove their data from the system.

Are their discounts for multiple licences?

Yes, re-sellers can also add additional discounts.

Are there any educational pricing or discounts?

Yes, please contact your nearest re-seller for further information.
