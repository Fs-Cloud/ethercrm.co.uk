Latest news and updates from EtherCRM

Binary01 has launched a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system EtherCRM.

The new CRM acts as a point of interaction between financial advisers and wealth managers and its customers.
The application contains the latest technologies and practices dedicated to Sales and Marketing and related to the business
combined with in-depth business processing workflows designed to improve regulatory compliance and relationships with customers.
It provides and compiles relevant information on them, and supports advisers and their teams in driving sales growth.
“We believe that it is crucial for businesses and customers within financial services to have a modern robust system, fit for purpose. A system which does not cost the earth and helps businesses grow. A system built with compliance in mind to help reduce risk. Enabling advisers and wealth managers to better serve their clients and raise industry standards.” - Edwin Luther, Founder EtherCRM is a customisable online CRM system, which helps you focus on what’s most important and lets you spend your energy on really making a difference.
